## Pair ladder and transfer set

1. `ב/כ` — transfer syllables: `בָּ`, `כָּ`; transfer words: `בַּלּוֹן`, `כַּדּוּר`
2. `ד/ר` — transfer syllables: `דָּ`, `רָ`; transfer words: `דָּג`, `רִמּוֹן`
3. `ו/ז` — transfer syllables: `וָ`, `זָ`; transfer words: `וֶרֶד`, `זֶבְרָה`
4. `ן/ו` (context contrast) — transfer syllable: `אָן`; transfer word anchor: `גַּן`
