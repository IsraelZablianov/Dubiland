## Scope and Method

- Audience: Hebrew-speaking parents of children ages 3-7 in Israel.
- Coverage target delivered: 50 keywords across Brand/Games/Letters/Numbers/Reading/Parent intent.
- Volume signal: Google Trends Israel (`today 12-m`) normalized demand score with anchor term `לימוד אותיות=100`.
- Difficulty proxy: Bing Hebrew SERP result counts (same-day pull) bucketed to Low/Medium/High competition.
- Note: a score of `0` means below Trends visibility threshold for exact-match query, not necessarily zero real searches.
- URL mapping aligns with public indexation architecture in `docs/architecture/2026-04-09-public-route-indexation-architecture.md` and planned `/games/*` expansion.

## Cannibalization Risks and Controls

1. `/letters` family overlap (`לימוד אותיות`, `לימוד אותיות לילדים`, `לימוד א-ב לגיל הרך`).
- Control: assign one primary keyword per page:
  - `/letters` -> `לימוד אותיות`
  - `/letters/kindergarten` -> `לימוד אותיות לגן חובה`
  - `/letters/prep-first-grade` -> `לימוד אותיות לכיתה א`
2. `/reading/prep-first-grade` overlap (`קריאה לכיתה א`, `לימוד קריאה לכיתה א`).
- Control: use `קריאה לכיתה א` as primary and keep the other term as secondary variant in H2/body.
3. `/games/numbers` overlap (`משחקי חשבון`, `משחקי מספרים`, `משחקי חשבון לגן`).
- Control: keep hub primary (`משחקי חשבון`) and route intent-specific terms to child pages (`/games/numbers/counting-picnic`, `/games/numbers/number-line-jumps`).
4. Parent readiness overlap (`גן חובה`, `הכנה לכיתה א`).
- Control: separate lifecycle intent:
  - `/parents/kindergarten-readiness` -> `גן חובה`
  - `/parents/first-grade-readiness` -> `הכנה לכיתה א`
